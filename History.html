HARP Grant Program

Application

All students living in residence halls are eligible for the program. The application consists of a grant proposal (pdf or word document) emailed to berkeleyharp@gmail.com due by March 7th. We will be holding grant writing workshops (see timeline) to assist with developing your idea. The grant proposal should be approximately 2 - 4 pages and consist of :

  1. A statement of the research question and the purpose of the research (175-200 words)
  2. The background on the research topic summarizing what is already known about this topic and how the research work you will be doing intends to build upon this knowledge (1-2 pages)
  3. The project plan for your research including a discussion of the research methods you will employ and a detailed timeline for your plan of action. Your timeline must show us that you are planning to carry out a minimum of 8 weeks of research.
  4. (optional) relevant experiences that prepare you to work on this project.
  5. Bibliography of any citations for your proposal
