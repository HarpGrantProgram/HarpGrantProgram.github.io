HARP Grant Program

Who We Are

At its inception, the team behind the HARP Grant Program consisted of just four students who have been engaged in the residence halls via various outlets. We've been active participants in the community through event planning, our own (small) projects and most of us are on hall staff and have seen the positive impact even small contributions can have. Given time, we hope to expand our executive team to a board of students that work with the Residence Hall staff in order to make the program more widely accessible to students.
